What Are the 10 Bodies of Consciousness?

In the ancient Kundalini Yoga tradition, passed down through Yogi Bhajan and beautifully articulated in Nirvair Singh Khalsa's teachings, we understand that consciousness exists in 10 distinct dimensions within each of us. These are not separate entities, but interconnected facets of our being that work together to create our experience of life.

Each body governs different aspects of how we perceive reality, make decisions, express ourselves, and experience the world. When these bodies are balanced and strong, we embody our highest potential. When they are weak or imbalanced, we experience struggle, confusion, and disconnection.

This sacred quiz is designed to help you discover which of your light bodies are shining brightest, and which are calling for attention and nurturing. There are no "good" or "bad" results—only a mirror reflecting where you are in this moment, and a gentle invitation to evolve.

For each body that shows an opportunity for growth, we offer a free practice that you can begin immediately. For deeper, guided work with kriyas, meditations, and expert mentorship, we invite you to join our community.
